Content Writer

You love writing and animals as well? Come to Aussie Wildlife where you can satisfy both of them.

To be more specific, your task is to do research and write weekly content related to the wildlife of Australia. Especially, you'll work directly with Ethan Dang (Head of Aussie Wildlife).

Training is provided.

Salary: $30/hour. Could vary

Event Organiser

Although organisation is created merely two months, it has gained certain interests from the public. Also, we are planning to hold several events in order to raise the public's awareness regarding the wildlife.

Working as an event organiser require candidates to equip themselves with good communication skills, an open mind and an "inexhaustible" source of enthusiasm. Preferably with experience.

Salary: $60/hour. Could vary

Media Manager

Our top priority is to reach out to the community and answer any questions they have. Thus, we are now hiring Media Manager to manage our website and social medias.

This position requires you to have decent knowledge and skillsets in using Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Canvas or any other designing platforms.

Salary: $30/hour. Could vary

Clothes Designer

We are also planning to sell merchandises as to raise funds for the wildlife and community events.

Interns are more than welcome to join us.

Salary: $40/hour. Could vary
